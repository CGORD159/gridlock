HOW TO PLAY
⛓️ BUILDING A CHAIN
1️⃣
Tap any tile to start. It glows and shows the number 1.
2️⃣
Tap or drag to touching tiles of the same colour. Up, down, left, right only.
🔙
Tap a tile already in your chain to undo back to that point.
⚡
Tap ⚡ CHAIN (or Space) to clear and score points.
❌
Wrong colour? Chain resets and starts from the new tile.
⚡ MULTIPLIERS
2 = 1×
3–4 = 2×
5–6 = 3×
7+ = 5× 🔥
