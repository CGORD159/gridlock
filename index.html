HOW TO PLAY
⛓️ BUILDING A CHAIN
1️⃣
Tap any tile to start your chain. The tile glows and shows the number 1.
2️⃣
Tap or drag to any touching tile of the same colour. Up, down, left, right — no diagonals. Each new tile gets a number.
🔙
Made a mistake? Tap any tile already in your chain to undo back to that point. You keep everything up to that tile.
⚡
Tap ⚡ CHAIN (or press Space/Enter) to clear your selected tiles and score points.
❌
Wrong colour or non-adjacent tile? The chain resets and starts fresh from that tile automatically.
⚡ SCORE MULTIPLIERS
The longer your chain, the higher your multiplier:
2 tiles = 1×
3–4 = 2×
5–6 = 3×
7+ = 5× 🔥 MEGA
A 7-tile chain scores 350 points. A 2-tile chain scores just 20. Always go longer!

❤️ LIVES, ROUNDS & LEVELLING UP
🎯
Each round you need to complete a target number of chains before the timer expires. The target is shown in the top right.
⏰
Timer runs out? You lose a life and the timer resets. Lose all 3 lives and it's game over.
📈
Complete the round target and you level up! Each level adds more colours to the grid and reduces the timer. How far can you go?
📅
Daily Challenge: Every day a unique grid is generated — the same for all players. Compare your score with friends!
