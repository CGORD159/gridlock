HOW TO PLAY
⛓️ BUILDING A CHAIN
1️⃣
Tap any tile to start — it glows and shows ①.
2️⃣
Tap or drag adjacent tiles of the same colour to extend.
🔙
Tap a tile already in chain to trim back to that point.
⚡
Tap ⚡ CHAIN (or Space) to clear all selected tiles and score.
⚡ MULTIPLIERS
2 = 1×
3–4 = 2×
5–6 = 3×
7+ = 5× 🔥
🆕 SPECIAL TILES
❄️
Frozen tiles — appear at higher levels. Must be included in a chain to unfreeze. They do not score until unfrozen.
✨
Wild tiles — match any colour. Including one in a chain activates +1 bonus multiplier on top of chain length.
💣
Bomb tiles — appear at level 6+. Committing a chain with a Bomb tile blasts all surrounding tiles as a bonus.
💡 POWER-UPS
💡
HINT 25🪙 — Highlights the biggest matching group.
🔀
SHUFFLE 40🪙 — Randomises all tiles when stuck.
💣
BOMB 60🪙 — Blasts all tiles of one colour.
⏱️
+TIME 50🪙 — Adds 8 seconds to the clock.
🔥 FEVER & BONUS ROUND
🌡️
Fever Mode — Making 3 chains quickly fills the Fever bar. In Fever, all multipliers are doubled for 8 seconds.
🎯
Bonus Round — Every 3 levels, a 15-second frenzy starts. Every tile cleared = +5 pts extra. No lives at risk.
❤️ LIVES & ECONOMY
⏰
Timer runs out = lose a life. Lose 3 lives = game over.
🪙
You earn a small number of coins by levelling up. Bigger rewards come from Daily Missions, achievements, and watching ads. Coins are scarce by design — they mean something!
